Why it matters

The deck is stacked against small business.

Large platforms promise reach, but take margin. Enterprise software promises efficiency, but charges enterprise prices. Local business owners end up dependent on systems designed by people who have never run a small shop, a family restaurant, or an independent store.

"Market dominance isn't inevitable – it's a design choice. We design the alternative."
  • ▶Independent retailers pay up to 30% in platform fees for sales they built themselves.
  • ▶Most SMB software is either too cheap to be reliable or too expensive to be accessible.
  • ▶Data from local businesses is used to compete against them. We don't do that.

How we work

From conversation to shipped software.

Step 01

Understand the business

We start by listening. No demos, no decks – just an honest conversation about how your business works, what slows it down, and what success looks like.

Step 02

Scope & propose

We produce a clear, plain-language proposal with timeline and cost. No jargon, no bait-and-switch pricing. You know exactly what you're getting.

Step 03

Build iteratively

You see working software early and often. We incorporate your feedback at every stage – not just at launch, when it's too late to change course.

Step 04

Hand off & support

When we're done, you own it outright – no lock-in. We stay available for support, updates, and future work on your terms.
