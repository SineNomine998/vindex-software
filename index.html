Questions & answers

Everything you might be wondering

How much does a project cost?

Every project is quoted up front with a fixed price, so there are no surprises. The cost depends on scope — a focused website is very different from a full management system. The first conversation is free, and so is the proposal.

How long does it take?

It depends on what we're building, but you'll get a clear timeline in your proposal before we start. We build iteratively and show you working software early, so you're never left wondering about progress.

Do I actually own the software?

Yes — 100%. When we hand over, the code and everything around it is yours outright. No lock-in, no leasing, no monthly licence just to keep using what you paid for.

What if I need changes after launch?

We stay available for updates, fixes, and new features whenever you need them — always on your terms. There's no forced support contract; you reach out when something comes up.

Where are you based?

We're a Netherlands-based studio working with local and growing businesses. We're happy to meet in person where practical, and work comfortably online too.

How do we get started?

Just send us a message describing your idea — no commitment, just a conversation. We'll get back to you within one business day to talk through what you need.

Ready to build something?

Tell us about your project — no commitment, just a conversation. We reply within one business day.
